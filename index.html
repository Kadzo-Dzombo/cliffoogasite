About Me

I am Major (Dr) Cliff Ooga Obwogi, a Researcher, Trainer and Lecturer in the Disciplines of Peace Support Operations, Media, Peace and Conflict Studies, Conflict/Crisis Management. I hold a Bachelor of Education Arts in English and Literature from Kenyatta University, a Diploma in Military Science from Egerton University, a Master of Arts in Communication Studies from the University of Nairobi and a PhD in Peace and Conflict Studies from Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology. I have vast experience in Media, Peace & Conflict studies and Peace Support Operations. I am currently working at the International Peace Support Training Centre as a Researcher and also a Lecturer of National Defence University-Kenya.
